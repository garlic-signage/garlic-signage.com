GarlicSignage - Open Source Software for Digital Signage

GarlicSignage is a robust, open-source platform built on the industry-standard SMIL language. Our mission is to provide you with the tools and documentation needed to create powerful and flexible digital signage solutions.

Our solution is built around two core, interconnected components that form a complete digital signage infrastructure:

  • Garlic Player: A versatile and lightweight media player that ensures full compatibility with the SMIL 3.0 standard, allowing you to seamlessly display your content.
  • Garlic Hub: A powerful, SMIL-based Content and Device Management System that provides the tools to efficiently manage and schedule your digital signage content.

Whether you're a developer looking for a reliable framework or a user ready to build stunning digital signage playlists, you'll find everything you need here:

  • For Users: Learn why SMIL is the smart choice for digital signage and dive into the essentials of creating captivating playlists with the Garlic Player.
  • For Developers: Get started with our apps and comprehensive documentation. You can also join our community and contribute to the project on GitHub.

Garlic Digital Signage gives you the freedom to build, customize, and deploy your solutions without proprietary limitations. Discover the power of open-source and the flexibility of SMIL today.
