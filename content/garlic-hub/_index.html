No lock-in. No forced cloud. Full control.

Garlic-Hub is a management solution for Digital Signage that covers core CMS tasks and device control. It creates playlists based on the industry standard SMIL and supports SMIL-compatible media players.

What Makes Garlic-Hub Unique?

  • Developer-Driven: Direct support from the makers, not chatbots or sales teams.
  • SMIL Compatibility: Uses the industry standard multimedia language SMIL for playlists and scheduling. This means open, interoperable, future-proof.
  • 100% Open Source: Entirely transparent, auditable, and adaptable. No “half open” compromise – the full system is open source.
  • Player Agnostic: Works with any SMIL-compatible media player, from Raspberry Pi to enterprise hardware.
  • Freedom of Deployment: Run it on-premise, in the cloud, or hybrid. You decide, not the vendor.
  • Privacy and Security: Garlic-Hub comes with no spyware, no advertising, and no user tracking.
