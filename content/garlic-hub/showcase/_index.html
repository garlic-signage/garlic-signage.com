Playlist Composer: Create Custom Playlists with Ease

The Playlist Composer gives you the power to create and customize playlists to your exact specifications.

  • Drag-and-Drop: Place videos or images into your playlist and move them.
  • Schedule:Set duration for your media, even for videos.
  • Nesting: Structure your content with nesting playlists.
  • Exports when finish to let the playlist get fetched from the player.

Multi-Zone: Layouts for Digital Signage Screens

SMIL players can divide your screen into multiple independent zones, each playing its own content. These zones can even overlap.

Imagine a large area displaying a main playlist, while a smaller, overlapping zone simultaneously runs a news ticker or promotional video. This means your digital signage can showcase multiple images, videos, or streams simultaneously, maximizing your advertising space.

Overlapping zones are perfect for dynamic effects like news tickers, picture-in-picture video, or stunning video wall displays.

Media pool: Your Central Hub for All Media

Say goodbye to scattered files and endless searching. Garlic-Hub's Medienpool provides a centralized, easy-to-access location for all your audio, video, and image assets. Upload once, and it's instantly available wherever you need it within the system.

  • Drag-and-Drop Uploader: Effortlessly add single or multiple files.
  • Folder & Category Management: Structure your content exactly how you want it.
  • Integrated Previews: View media directly within the dashboard.

Uploader

The Garlic-Hub Uploader offers versatile options for bringing your content into the system.

  • Local Files: Easily upload files from your device using a simple drag-and-drop interface.
  • External Links: Import media directly by providing a URL from external sources.
  • Webcam Capture: Instantly create and upload photos or videos directly from your webcam.
  • Screen Recording: Capture screen activity and upload it as a video file or screenshot.
  • Stock Media Integration: Access and import images and videos via API key from Unsplash, Pixabay, and Pexels.
